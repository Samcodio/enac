About ENAC?

ENAC is an agency that connects students or aspiring students to owners of vacant lodges or to other students in need of roommates.

ENAC works by granting access to anyone looking to lease out their lodges, granted they submit the required the information which is then reviewed by our workers.

Payment is made through paystack, a government certified fintech agency. Paymment is protected and all methods of payments are permitted.

Enac was founded by Oguegbu David Izuchukwu. Studied at Chukwuemeka Odumegwu Ojukwu University, Uli. ENAC is in it's prime and is expected to rise to greater heights
